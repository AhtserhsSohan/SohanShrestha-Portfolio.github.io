Project 1: Bing Map Integration

In this project, I have integrated the Bing Map. The Bing Map consists of two pushpins: one is positioned at the default location of Sydney, while the other is placed according to the user's location. The application requests the user's permission to access their location, and then pins the pushpin accordingly. Users can check the longitude and latitude of their location by tapping on the pushpin labeled 'User'.
